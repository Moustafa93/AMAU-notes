## The Pathways - المسارات

The student of knowledge program consists of an introductory course, followed by 7 pathways
1. ‘Aqeedah and the Foundations of the Religion
2. Fiqh & its Principles
3. Tafseer & the Sciences of the Quran
4. Hadith & its Sciences
5. Seerah & History
6. Science of Language and Logic
7. Islamic Etiquette & Personal Development

Must at least begin Aqeedah pathway.

If a student has the ability to add a second pathway, it is recommended to do Aqeedah and Fiqh.

---

## Levels of Study

Each pathways is divided into 4 levels:
- Introduction
- Beginner
- Intermediate
- Advanced

The introduction consists of introducing the subject matter, the key terms and definitions etc.

The later levels consist of classical books.

No requirement to complete all pathways before moving to a higher level in one particular pathway, with the exception of the final advanced level - before embarking on any pathway at the advanced level, all pathways must be completed to the intermediate level.

---

## Memorisation

For each pathway is a memorization pathway, consisting of key books in the subject

There is an easy and a hard route depending on ability

There is also a Quran memorisation schedule, based on the level of the pathways that the student has reached:
- By end of beginner level - 5 Juz
- By end of intermediate level - 15 Juz
- By end of advanced level - 30 Juz

---

## Time Requirements

This is a flexible program

More or less time can be dedicated as per the ability and circumstances of the student. And time can be increased and decreased depending on changes in circumstances.

In general, each pathway requires:
- ~2 hours per week of lectures
- ~4 hours per week of self directed study (review, organising notes etc)

This program will take many years to complete
